## Description (4000 char max)

Dream Shepherd is a dream journal for Christians who want to tend to their dreams the way Scripture's dreamers did. Capture your dreams before they fade. Receive thoughtful, biblically grounded interpretations. Track patterns over time and discover what the Shepherd may be showing you in the night.

"For God speaks in one way, and in two, though man does not perceive it. In a dream, in a vision of the night." (Job 33:14-15)

Throughout Scripture, dreams carried meaning. Joseph interpreted Pharaoh's. Daniel discerned the king's. Jacob saw the ladder. Joseph the husband of Mary received guidance in his sleep. Peter saw the sheet from heaven. Dream Shepherd stands in that lineage as a thoughtful companion for the modern dreamer.

FEATURES

• AI dream interpretation rooted in biblical wisdom and depth psychology
• Scripture references woven naturally into symbol meanings
• Pattern insights across your dreams over weeks and months
• Lucid dreaming tools and reality checks
• Daily streak tracker to build the habit of recording
• Private journal by default. Opt-in community for sharing
• Beautiful design inspired by illuminated manuscripts and starlit nights
• Works offline. Syncs when you reconnect
• Export your dreams as a beautifully formatted PDF

A QUIET COMPANION

Dream Shepherd is not a pastor on a pulpit. It is a gentle interpreter that draws on Scripture, symbolism, and modern dream psychology to help you see what your dreams may be saying. We never moralize, never assume crisis, and never preach. The Shepherd's voice is quiet.

PRIVACY

Your dreams are yours. Entries are private by default. You choose what to share, what to keep, and what to delete. Your account can be permanently deleted from the app at any time.

WHO IT IS FOR

Anyone who believes their dreams carry meaning worth listening to. Christians who want a journal that takes Scripture seriously. Seekers curious about what dreams may reveal. Those who have lost a recent dream and wish they had written it down sooner.

FREE TIER

Five free AI interpretations to begin. Earn more by sharing dreams with the community. Unlimited interpretations and additional features available with Dream Shepherd Pro.

A NOTE ON SCRIPTURE

Dream interpretation in the Bible was never formulaic. Sometimes a dream was prophetic. Sometimes it was psychological. Sometimes it was simply the soul speaking to itself in the only language it knew. Dream Shepherd treats every dream with that same reverence and care.
