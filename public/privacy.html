Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Dream Shepherd is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and, if you use social sign-in (Google or Apple), your name and profile picture as provided by the authentication provider.

Dream Journal Data: The dreams you record, including descriptions, titles, moods, themes, tags, characters, lucid dream details, and any other information you enter into the journal.

AI-Generated Content: Interpretations, themes, and visualizations generated from your dreams are stored alongside your dream entries.

Usage Data: We collect basic usage information such as interpretation count, dream count, and feature usage to provide app functionality like pattern tracking and statistics.

Payment Information: If you subscribe, payment is processed through Stripe. We do not store your credit card details. We store only your Stripe customer ID to manage your subscription.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and operate the dream journaling service
  • To generate AI-powered dream interpretations and visualizations
  • To track your dream patterns and statistics
  • To display community-shared dreams (only dreams you choose to share)
  • To process subscription payments
  • To send dream reminder notifications (if you enable them)
  • To improve the App and develop new features

3. AI Processing

When you request a dream interpretation or visualization, your dream description and related metadata are sent to a third-party AI service for processing. This data is used solely to generate your interpretation or image and is not used to train AI models. We do not share your dream content with any other third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides encrypted database storage and secure authentication. All data transmission between your device and our servers uses HTTPS encryption.

5. Your Dreams Are Private

All dreams are private by default. Only you can see your dream journal. If you choose to share a dream with the community, other users can see the dream content you shared. You can unshare any dream at any time, which removes it from the community feed.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data or dream content to third parties. We share data only with:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication provider
  • AI service providers: For generating interpretations and visualizations (dream content only, processed per-request)
  • Stripe: For payment processing (payment data only)
  • Vercel: For hosting the application

7. Data Retention

Your dream data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed from our systems. Community posts from deleted accounts may be retained in anonymized form.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your dream data at any time through the App
  • Export your dream journal data
  • Delete individual dreams or your entire account
  • Unshare any community posts
  • Opt out of dream reminder notifications

9. Children's Privacy

Dream Shepherd is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Cookies and Tracking

Dream Shepherd uses essential cookies and local storage for authentication and app functionality. We do not use third-party advertising trackers or analytics cookies.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the App. Continued use after changes are posted constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at support@dreamshepherd.app.
